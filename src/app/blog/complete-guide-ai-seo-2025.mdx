Ensure your critical content is available in the initial HTML response without requiring JavaScript execution. Additionally, avoid unnecessary meta robots "noindex" directives - only use these for content you genuinely don't want AI systems to access, as they completely prevent AI visibility.</p>

      <h3>Content Structure Problems</h3>
      <p>Burying key information behind lengthy introductions frustrates both users and AI systems. Lead with answers rather than building up to your main points, as AI systems prioritize content that provides immediate value. Vague headings like "Getting Started" or "Our Approach" provide no context for AI understanding - use descriptive headings that clearly indicate content topic and purpose.</p>

      <p>Wall-of-text content without structure makes it difficult for AI systems to parse and extract information. Use headings, lists, and paragraph breaks to create scannable structure that both humans and AI can navigate effectively. Finally, avoid focusing on keyword density over user intent - provide comprehensive, helpful information that genuinely serves your audience rather than trying to game algorithmic systems.</p>
